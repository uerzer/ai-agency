# AI Agency Cold Outreach Email Sequence

> **Purpose:** 5-email sequence for an AI automation agency targeting local businesses
> that lack modern digital tools (online booking, chatbots, automated follow-up).
>
> **Cadence:** Day 0 → Day 3 → Day 7 → Day 14 → Day 21
>
> **Merge Fields:** `{business_name}`, `{owner_name}`, `{city}`, `{niche}`,
> `{competitor_name}`, `{website_url}`, `{specific_observation}`, `{loom_url}`,
> `{sender_name}`, `{sender_company}`, `{calendar_link}`

---

## Email 1 — Day 0: The Pattern Interrupt

**Subject line options (A/B test):**
- A: `I found something costing {business_name} money`
- B: `Quick question about {business_name}'s website`
- C: `{owner_name}, your competitors are doing this — you're not`

**Body:**

```
Hi {owner_name},

I was looking for a {niche} in {city} and came across {business_name}.

I noticed a few things that are probably costing you customers right now:

{specific_observation}

Meanwhile, {competitor_name} down the road has online booking, a chatbot
that answers questions at 2 AM, and automated review requests — so when
someone searches "{niche} near me," they're stacking the deck.

This isn't a criticism. Most {niche}s haven't caught up yet, which is
exactly why the ones that move first are cleaning up.

I help {niche}s in {city} add AI-powered booking, chatbots, and follow-up
systems. Takes about a week to set up. No long contracts.

Worth a 15-minute call to see if it makes sense?

{calendar_link}

Either way, hope business is going well.

— {sender_name}
{sender_company}
```

**Why it works:**
- Opens with *their* business, not yours — pattern interrupt vs. typical sales pitch
- Names a specific competitor — creates urgency without being aggressive
- Lists concrete gaps (booking, chatbot, reviews) — not vague "digital transformation"
- Low-commitment CTA (15-min call, no contracts)

## Email 2 — Day 3: The Case Study

**Subject line options:**
- A: `How a {niche} in {city} added $12K/mo with one AI tool`
- B: `This {niche} went from 4 to 47 online bookings per week`
- C: `Re: {business_name}` *(reply-style, high open rate)*

**Body:**

```
Hi {owner_name},

Quick follow-up — wanted to share a real example since it's directly
relevant to {business_name}.

We worked with a {niche} in {city} who had the same setup you have now:
- Phone-only booking (they were missing 60% of after-hours calls)
- No way to capture website visitors (people would browse and leave)
- Reviews trickling in at 1-2 per month

Here's what we installed in 5 business days:

1. AI BOOKING ASSISTANT — Handles scheduling 24/7 via their website
   and Google listing. No more phone tag.

2. SMART CHATBOT — Answers the top 20 questions their receptionist
   gets asked every day (pricing, hours, insurance, parking). Captures
   name + phone for every visitor.

3. AUTOMATED REVIEW ENGINE — After each appointment, sends a friendly
   text asking for a Google review. They went from 43 to 187 reviews
   in 90 days.

Result: $12,400/mo in new revenue from patients who would have gone
to a competitor.

I'm not saying you'll get the exact same result — but the gap between
where {business_name} is now and where it could be is significant.

Happy to walk you through exactly what this would look like for your
practice. No pitch, just a screen share.

{calendar_link}

— {sender_name}
```

**Why it works:**
- Specific numbers ($12K/mo, 60% missed calls, 43→187 reviews) build credibility
- "Same setup you have now" — makes the case study feel personal
- Three deliverables are tangible and easy to understand
- CTA is framed as a screen share, not a "sales call"

## Email 3 — Day 7: The Loom Video

**Subject line options:**
- A: `I recorded a 2-min video about {business_name}'s website`
- B: `Made this for you — 2 min video`
- C: `{owner_name}, take a look at this`

**Body:**

```
Hi {owner_name},

Instead of another text email, I recorded a quick 2-minute video
walking through {business_name}'s website and showing exactly what
I'd change:

👉 {loom_url}

In the video I cover:
- The #1 thing visitors see that's making them leave
- Where to add a booking widget (takes 10 minutes)
- A chatbot demo showing how it would work on YOUR site
- How to automatically get 5x more Google reviews

No strings attached — even if we never work together, the tips in
the video will help.

If anything resonates, grab 15 minutes here: {calendar_link}

Talk soon,
{sender_name}
```

**Why it works:**
- Loom videos have 3-5x the response rate of text-only emails
- Proves you actually looked at THEIR website (not mass email)
- Gives free value upfront — builds reciprocity and trust
- "Even if we never work together" removes pressure

**Loom video script (record for each lead):**

> 1. Open their website. "Hey {owner_name}, I pulled up {business_name}'s
>    site — let me show you a few quick wins."
> 2. Point out the homepage: "Right now a visitor lands here and the only
>    option is to call. What about the 70% of people who won't pick up
>    the phone?"
> 3. Show a chatbot mockup: "Imagine this sitting in the corner — answers
>    questions, captures leads, books appointments."
> 4. Show booking widget: "This would go right here. Syncs with your
>    calendar. Patient picks a time, done."
> 5. Close: "That's it — 3 changes, maybe a week of work. Happy to chat
>    if you want to explore it."

## Email 4 — Day 14: Social Proof + Urgency

**Subject line options:**
- A: `3 {niche}s in {city} signed up this week`
- B: `{city} {niche}s are moving fast on this`
- C: `Quick update (then I'll stop bugging you)`

**Body:**

```
{owner_name},

Quick update — 3 {niche}s in the {city} area signed up for our AI
system this week.

I don't say that to pressure you. I say it because once your
competitors have these tools running, the gap gets harder to close.
The business that shows up first with online booking, instant chat
responses, and 200+ Google reviews wins the "near me" search.

Here's what's happening in your market right now:

- 72% of patients/customers book online when the option exists
- Businesses with AI chatbots capture 3x more leads after hours
- The average {niche} with 150+ reviews gets 2x more calls from Google

We're keeping our onboarding small — 5 businesses per month in each
city so we can deliver white-glove setup. We have 2 slots open for
{city} this month.

No pressure at all, but if you've been thinking about it:

{calendar_link}

— {sender_name}
{sender_company}
```

**Why it works:**
- Local social proof ("3 businesses in YOUR city") is more powerful than national stats
- Scarcity is real and justified ("5 per month per city")
- Market stats validate the urgency without being pushy
- "No pressure" + "2 slots" creates tension that drives action

  - AI receptionist (handles phone calls you miss)

  - Reputation management dashboard
  - Priority support

Both include full setup (no extra fee) and a 30-day money-back

{calendar_link}

- Leaves the door open with no pressure
- 30-day guarantee removes risk

## Sending Best Practices

1. **Send window:** Tuesday-Thursday, 7:30-9:00 AM local time
2. **Volume:** Max 30 new leads/day when warming a new domain
3. **Domain:** Use a separate sending domain (e.g., `try-{company}.com`)
4. **Warm-up:** 2 weeks of gradual volume increase before full sends
5. **Tracking:** Use UTM parameters on calendar links for attribution
6. **Reply handling:** Respond to all replies within 2 hours during business hours
7. **Personalization:** ALWAYS record the Loom video — never skip Email 3
8. **Compliance:** Include physical address and unsubscribe link (CAN-SPAM)
